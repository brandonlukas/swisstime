Lido

A Swiss-style workout timer. Every finished workout drops a toy in your pool.

Support

Found a bug, or have an idea? Email brandonlukas@gmail.com — you'll be talking to the person who made it.

Is my data backed up?

Everything Lido knows lives on your device and rides along in your normal iCloud or computer backups. Restore or transfer to a new phone and your workouts and pool history come with you. Deleting the app deletes its data.

Why does audio keep playing with the screen locked?

During a running workout, Lido keeps its audio session alive so spoken cues and beeps reach you with the phone locked or in your pocket. It ends when the workout does.
