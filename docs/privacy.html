Privacy Policy

Lido · Effective July 4, 2026

Lido collects no data. None.

What we collect

Nothing. Lido has no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, no accounts, and no third-party software of any kind. The app makes no network connections — there is no server for your data to be sent to.

Where your data lives

Your workouts, workout history, and settings are stored only on your device. They are included in your normal device backups (iCloud backup or a computer backup), so they move with you to a new phone when you restore or transfer. Deleting the app deletes this data.

Permissions

Lido requests no permissions. Spoken workout cues are generated on your device by Apple's built-in speech synthesis.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this address with a new effective date. Since Lido collects nothing, there is very little that could change.
